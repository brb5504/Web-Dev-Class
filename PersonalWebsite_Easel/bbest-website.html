WHO WE ARE

Easel is a trailblazer in Caribbean education and career transformation, targeting Micro, Small and Medium business owners and professionals, providing training in eCommerce, digital marketing and UX/UI (User Experience/User Interface) best practices.

Easel is a trailblazer in Caribbean education and career transformation, targeting Micro, Small and Medium business owners and professionals, providing training in eCommerce, digital marketing and UX/UI (User Experience/User Interface) best practices.

Our lofty goal is to become the Caribbean’s go-to resource for professionals interested in eCommerce and its entire ecosystem, eventually building out a “Community-as-a-resource,” strategy that spans the Caribbean diaspora.

Launching in Barbados (2018), with plans to export our successful curriculum throughout the Caribbean to include regional partnerships with Trinidad, St. Lucia, St. Vincent, Grenada, Guyana, Jamaica, and eventually countries in Africa.
